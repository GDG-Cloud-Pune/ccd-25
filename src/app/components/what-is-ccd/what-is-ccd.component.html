What is CCD?

Cloud Community Days Pune 2025 is back for its fourth edition—bigger, better, and more hands-on than ever. This year, we’re bringing together developers, cloud architects, AI enthusiasts, and tech professionals for a full-day experience packed with insightful talks, real-world case studies, and lightning sessions that spark ideas and share learnings from the field.

What’s new? We’re introducing immersive, workshops designed to help you roll up your sleeves and build on the cloud. Whether you’re here to explore scalable architectures, dive into AI/ML, or enhance your security posture, there’s something for everyone. Choose to attend just the conference, just the workshops, or go all-in with an all-access pass—your cloud journey, your way.
